SOP Ra/Vao Cong Doanh Nghiep (Ap Dung Ngay)
Tai lieu: SOP-GATE-ACCESS-001
Phien ban: 1.0
Hieu luc tu: 08/03/2026
Don vi ap dung: [Ten doanh nghiep]

1. Muc tieu
- Chuan hoa quy trinh dang ky, phe duyet, kiem soat nguoi ra/vao cong.
- Dam bao an ninh, truy vet day du, giam thoi gian xu ly tai cong.
- Ro trach nhiem theo tung vai tro: Requester, Approver, Security, Admin.

2. Pham vi
- Ap dung cho: khach, nha thau, ung vien, giao hang, doi tac, nhan su thoi vu.
- Khong ap dung cho: nhan su noi bo dung the nhan vien hop le (tru khu vuc han che yeu cau phe duyet rieng).

3. Thuat ngu trang thai
- DRAFT: Yeu cau nhap.
- PENDING_APPROVAL: Cho duyet.
- APPROVED: Da duyet.
- REJECTED: Tu choi.
- CHECKED_IN: Da vao cong.
- CHECKED_OUT: Da ra cong.
- OVERDUE: Qua gio nhung chua check-out.
- CANCELLED: Huy yeu cau.

4. SLA chuan
- Tao yeu cau truoc gio den toi thieu 2 gio.
- Thoi gian duyet cap 1: <= 30 phut.
- Thoi gian xu ly tai cong moi luot: <= 60 giay.
- Dong phien check-out cuoi ngay: truoc 23:59.
- Xu ly ban ghi OVERDUE: trong 15 phut ke tu canh bao.

5. Du lieu bat buoc
- Ho ten khach.
- Loai giay to: CCCD/Passport.
- So giay to.
- So dien thoai.
- Don vi/cong ty.
- Muc dich den.
- Nguoi phu trach noi bo.
- Thoi gian du kien vao/ra.
- Bien so xe (neu co).
- Khu vuc duoc phep vao.

6. RACI theo vai tro
- Requester: chiu trach nhiem tao va cap nhat chinh xac thong tin.
- Approver: chiu trach nhiem phe duyet/tu choi dung chinh sach.
- Security: chiu trach nhiem xac minh tai cong va ghi nhan check-in/out.
- Admin: chiu trach nhiem cau hinh he thong, giam sat log, bao cao, xu ly su co.

7. Quy trinh tong the (E2E)
1. Requester tao yeu cau.
2. He thong validate du lieu, blacklist, trung lich.
3. Approver duyet hoac tu choi.
4. Khi duyet, he thong phat hanh QR/token co han.
5. Security check-in tai cong.
6. Theo doi luu tru, canh bao qua gio.
7. Security check-out khi khach roi cong.
8. Admin doi soat cuoi ngay va xu ly ngoai le.

8. SOP chi tiet theo tung vai tro

8.1 SOP cho Requester
Muc tieu: Tao yeu cau dung, du, dung thoi gian.

Quy trinh thao tac:
1. Dang nhap he thong bang tai khoan ca nhan.
2. Chon Tao yeu cau ra/vao cong.
3. Nhap du du lieu bat buoc.
4. Chon muc uu tien: Thuong hoac Khan.
5. Dinh kem tai lieu neu can: thu moi, hop dong, lenh cong tac.
6. Xac nhan cam ket thong tin chinh xac.
7. Gui yeu cau sang trang thai PENDING_APPROVAL.
8. Theo doi trang thai.
9. Neu bi REJECTED, chinh sua va gui lai.
10. Neu ke hoach thay doi, chu dong CANCELLED truoc gio hen.

Checklist truoc khi gui:
- Dung so giay to.
- Dung nguoi phu trach noi bo.
- Dung khung gio den.
- Dung khu vuc duoc phep vao.
- Co bien so xe neu khach di o to/xe may.

Diem kiem soat:
- Khong tao ho so thay nguoi khac bang tai khoan dung chung.
- Khong dung du lieu giay to gia/thieu can cu.
- Khong gui yeu cau khan tru truong hop that su can.

8.2 SOP cho Approver
Muc tieu: Duyet dung chinh sach, dung thoi gian.

Quy trinh thao tac:
1. Mo danh sach PENDING_APPROVAL.
2. Kiem tra muc dich, tinh hop le, rui ro.
3. Doi chieu blacklist va lich su vi pham (neu co).
4. Ra quyet dinh.
5. Chon APPROVED neu hop le.
6. Chon REJECTED va ghi ro ly do neu khong hop le.
7. Voi ho so nhay cam, chuyen duyet cap 2 (An ninh/HCNS) theo chinh sach.
8. Bao dam quyet dinh trong SLA <= 30 phut.

Tieu chi phe duyet:
- Co nguoi phu trach noi bo ro rang.
- Muc dich cong viec hop le.
- Khung gio phu hop chinh sach cong ty.
- Khong thuoc danh sach han che/blacklist.

Diem kiem soat:
- Khong duyet thieu thong tin.
- Khong duyet ngoai chinh sach neu khong co phe duyet dac biet.
- Moi tu choi phai co ly do bang van ban tren he thong.

8.3 SOP cho Security (Bao ve cong)
Muc tieu: Check-in/check-out nhanh, dung chuan an ninh.

Quy trinh check-in:
1. Yeu cau khach xuat trinh QR hoac giay to dinh danh.
2. Quet QR hoac tra cuu bang so giay to/so dien thoai.
3. Doi chieu anh, ho ten, giay to that.
4. Neu khop va trang thai APPROVED, thuc hien CHECKED_IN.
5. In/cap the khach tam (neu co).
6. Huong dan khach khu vuc duoc phep va quy dinh an toan.
7. Ghi nhan truong hop dac biet vao nhat ky ca truc.

Quy trinh check-out:
1. Thu hoi the khach tam.
2. Quet xac nhan roi cong.
3. Cap nhat trang thai CHECKED_OUT.
4. Neu khach ra qua gio, gan co OVERDUE_RESOLVED va ghi chu ly do.

Tu choi cho vao khi:
- Khong co ho so APPROVED.
- Giay to khong khop voi ho so.
- QR het han/da dung sai quy tac.
- Co canh bao an ninh muc cao.

Diem kiem soat:
- Khong cho vao bang xac nhan mieng.
- Khong bo qua buoc doi chieu giay to.
- Khong dung tai khoan ca truc cua nguoi khac.

8.4 SOP cho Admin
Muc tieu: Dam bao he thong on dinh, du lieu chuan, bao cao day du.

Nhiem vu hang ngay:
1. Kiem tra dashboard van hanh dau ngay.
2. Theo doi canh bao OVERDUE, tu choi bat thuong, loi quet.
3. Doi soat cuoi ngay:
4. Danh sach chua CHECKED_OUT.
5. Danh sach check-in khong QR.
6. Danh sach chinh sua ho so sau phe duyet.
7. Xuat bao cao ngay cho An ninh/HCNS.

Nhiem vu hang tuan:
1. Ra soat phan quyen tai khoan.
2. Kiem tra log truy cap bat thuong.
3. Kiem tra SLA duyet va SLA cong.
4. Hop cai tien voi Security va bo phan lien quan.

Nhiem vu hang thang:
1. Tong hop KPI.
2. De xuat cap nhat chinh sach.
3. Kiem thu quy trinh du phong khi mat mang/mat dien.

Diem kiem soat:
- Audit log phai luu day du, khong chinh sua xoa thu cong.
- Tai khoan nhay cam bat buoc MFA.
- Sao luu du lieu theo chinh sach IT.

9. SOP ngoai le

9.1 Khach khong co QR
1. Security tao ho so tam tai cong.
2. Goi xac nhan nguoi phu trach noi bo.
3. Approver cap quyen khan tren he thong.
4. Security moi cho check-in.
5. Gan co MANUAL_ENTRY de audit.

9.2 Quen giay to
1. Tu choi check-in.
2. Cho phep cho tai khu vuc tiep nhan.
3. Chi xu ly tiep khi co giay to hop le hoac phe duyet dac biet cap an ninh.

9.3 Mat mang/mat he thong
1. Chuyen sang so tay offline mau chuan.
2. Ghi day du thoi gian, giay to, nguoi xac nhan.
3. Dong bo lai he thong trong <= 2 gio sau khi khoi phuc.
4. Admin doi soat ban ghi offline va online.

9.4 Bao dong an ninh
1. Security khoa luong check-in moi.
2. Thong bao ngay cho quan ly an ninh.
3. Thuc hien quy trinh khan cap cua doanh nghiep.
4. Luu bien ban su co va timeline xu ly.

10. KPI van hanh
- Ty le check-in xu ly trong <= 60 giay: muc tieu >= 95%.
- Ty le duyet dung SLA: muc tieu >= 98%.
- Ty le ban ghi OVERDUE: muc tieu <= 1%.
- Ty le ho so bi tu choi do thieu du lieu: muc tieu <= 3%.
- Ty le log day du cho giao dich nhay cam: muc tieu 100%.

11. Bieu mau bat buoc
- FM-01: Phieu dang ky khach/doi tac.
- FM-02: Phieu xac nhan ngoai le tai cong.
- FM-03: Nhat ky ca truc Security.
- FM-04: Bao cao doi soat cuoi ngay.
- FM-05: Bao cao su co an ninh.

12. Quy dinh tuan thu va ky luat
- Vi pham bo qua buoc xac minh giay to: xu ly theo noi quy an ninh.
- Chia se tai khoan hoac thao tac ho khong uy quyen: xu ly ky luat.
- Co y nhap sai du lieu: xu ly theo quy che cong ty va phap luat hien hanh.

13. Ke hoach trien khai trong 7 ngay
1. Ngay 1: Ban hanh SOP va truyen thong noi bo.
2. Ngay 2: Cau hinh he thong theo vai tro.
3. Ngay 3: Dao tao Requester + Approver.
4. Ngay 4: Dao tao Security theo checklist ca truc.
5. Ngay 5: Chay thu UAT tai 1 cong.
6. Ngay 6: Sua loi quy trinh, chot bieu mau.
7. Ngay 7: Go-live toan bo va theo doi sat 48 gio.
